Who I Am & What I Build

I'm a Backend Developer with solid experience building enterprise-grade systems using .NET, C#, and the Microsoft Azure ecosystem. My focus is on writing clean, maintainable code that solves real business problems at scale.

I care deeply about system design: how data flows, how services communicate, how failures are handled gracefully, and how performance can be improved without sacrificing correctness. I approach every project with an architectural mindset, not just an implementation one.

Beyond writing code, I bring experience working with cross-functional teams, understanding business requirements, and translating them into technical solutions that are both pragmatic and built to last.
